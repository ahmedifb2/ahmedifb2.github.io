Frequently Asked Questions

What is VLESS protocol?
VLESS is a lightweight, secure, and multiplexing proxy protocol designed for high-performance scenarios. It's known for its efficiency and low latency, making it perfect for modern internet usage.
Is AHMED VPN free to use?
We offer a free trial version with limited features, but our full-featured premium plans provide the best experience with unlimited bandwidth and advanced security features.
How many devices can I use with one account?
Our free plan allows one device, while the Pro plan supports up to 5 devices simultaneously. Enterprise plans offer unlimited device connections.
Does AHMED keep logs of my activity?
Absolutely not! We have a strict no-logs policy. Your online activities, browsing history, and personal information are never recorded or stored.
What platforms does AHMED support?
AHMED works on all major platforms including Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, and iOS. We also provide browser extensions for Chrome and Firefox.
How do I get VLESS configuration files?
You can easily generate VLESS configuration files through our app or website once you've subscribed to one of our plans. The configurations can be imported into compatible clients like Xray or V2Ray.
